Rules
  • 2024 batch pass-outs may also register.
  • Registrations must be completed by 23rd October.
  • Pitch it! is divided into 2 rounds.
  • Round 1: Online submission of elevator pitch and PPT. The elevator pitch is a 1-minute pitch explaining the idea.
  • The submission link will be provided to the team leader after registration.
  • Online submission must be done by 23rd October.
  • Only the best 10 teams will qualify for round 2.
  • Round 2: Offline presentation. Each team will get 5 minutes to pitch their idea/product and 5 minutes for a Q&A session.
  • Participants need to prepare a PPT to present their idea.
  • Each presentation should include:
    • Problem Statement
    • Solution
    • Market Analysis
    • Revenue Model
    • Challenges
    • Future Scope
  • NOTE: Participants may choose any method they prefer to convey their presentation.
  • Teams can present a prototype to further convince the judges.
